About Me

I'm a postgraduate student at the University of Durham, studying for a PhD within the Centre for Particle Theory. My supervisors are Professors Mohamed Anber and Iñaki García-Etxebarria. I completed my MSc in Particles, Strings and Cosmology and my undergraduate degree in Natural Sciences (Mathematics and Computer Science) also at the University of Durham. My full CV can be found here.

My MSc Thesis was titled 'Generalised Symmetry and Geometric Engineering', supervised by Professor Iñaki García-Etxebarria. My undergraduate thesis was titled 'Special Holonomy Manifolds in String Theory and M-Theory Reductions', supervised by Professors A.P. Braun and A. Donos.
