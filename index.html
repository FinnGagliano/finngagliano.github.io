About Me

I'm a postgraduate student at the University of Durham, studying for an MSc in Particles, Strings and Cosmology within the Centre for Particle Theory. Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate MSc in Mathematics and Computer Science also at the University of Durham. My full CV can be found here.

Research Interests

My main interests lie in geometrical perspectives of string theory and M-theory, but I also enjoy geometry independently:

  • Calabi-Yau Manifolds in String Theory
  • G2 Manifolds in M-Theory
  • Sasaki-Einstein Manifolds in the AdS/CFT Correspondence
